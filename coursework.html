Digital Media Arts 106: Animation

I had some experience with 2D animation, but none with 3D.This was my first time using 3D modelling programs like Maya and Blender!

Japanese 1

Mari Brunson

I had taken 5 years of Japanese before this class, but I recommend it to anyone just starting to learn a difficult language like Japanese! It was nice to practice with other people for the first time in a long while. Mari helped me perfect my emphasis and accent."

Art 120: Drawing 1

I was already an experienced traditional artist, but Dale taught me to appreciate the little victories in art, and to always keep a critical eye.
